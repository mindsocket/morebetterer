About

Welcome to More Betterer. This site lets you vote on images. It is inspired by, and in the style of, Best Thing, The Fairest etc by XKCD. Images from my Photo of the Day series are presented to visitors in pairs, and a "winner" in each challenge is chosen each time. Based on the collected results, a theoretical ranking of the most popular photos can be obtained. It uses a similar approach to the above sites, but I am experimenting with alternative ranking systems.

Apart from satisfying my own curiosity as to which of my photos are popular, this project is my first foray into using the django web framework.

Feel free to send any comments by email to me (roger) at my domain (mindsocket.com.au). If you like a particular photo, high quality prints are available in various forms (from affordable greeting cards through to fancy framed versions) from Red Bubble.

Enjoy!
- Rog
